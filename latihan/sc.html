School!

A pondok is a simple dormitory or living place where students, known as santri, stay while studying—especially in Islamic boarding schools called pesantren. The word pondok comes from the Arabic term “funduq,” which means inn or lodging. In Indonesia, the pondok is closely connected to the pesantren system, which is a traditional Islamic educational institution led by a kyai (Islamic scholar). Within the pondok, students not only study religious knowledge—such as Qur’anic interpretation (tafsir), Hadith, Islamic law (fiqh), theology (tauhid), and ethics (akhlaq)—but also learn discipline, independence, and moral values. Life in a pondok teaches simplicity, humility, cooperation, and devotion to Allah. It is not only a place to gain knowledge, but also a place to build character and spiritual maturity.

Lesson

Tajwid

Tajwid :adalah ilmu yang mempelajari cara membaca huruf-huruf Al-Qur’an dengan benar, sesuai makhraj (tempat keluarnya huruf) dan sifatnya.

Tahfidz

Tahfidz :adalah kegiatan menghafal ayat-ayat Al-Qur’an dengan benar, serta menjaga hafalan itu melalui pengulangan (muraja’ah) secara rutin.

Fiqih

Fiqih :adalah ilmu yang mempelajari hukum-hukum Islam yang bersumber dari Al-Qur’an dan Hadis, meliputi cara beribadah, bermuamalah, dan berakhlak.

IT

IT :adalah bidang yang mempelajari, menggunakan, dan mengembangkan sistem komputer untuk mengolah dan mengamankan data.
